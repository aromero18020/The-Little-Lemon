About Us

Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean
restaurant, focused on traditional recipes using
natural garden-fresh herbs and vegetables served
with a modern twist. Little Lemon provides guests
with a fresh, clean, and relaxed rustic atmosphere
making it the perfect place to wine and dine!
